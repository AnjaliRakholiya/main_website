Weight Concept

Baggage Dimensions for First and Business

  • The sum of the 3 dimensions (length + breadth + height) of each piece must not exceed 62 inches or 158 centimetres for each piece.
  • The maximum weight permissible for a single piece of baggage is 32 kgs. or 70 pounds.

Baggage Dimensions for Economy

  • For Economy passengers with two pieces of luggage, the total combined dimensions (length + breadth + height) of both pieces should not exceed 273cm (107 inches). Also the linear dimension of each piece should not exceed 62 inches.
  • The weight of each bag must not exceed 23 Kgs or 50 pounds.

Piece Concept

Baggage Dimensions for First and Business

  • The sum of the 3 dimensions (length + breadth + height) must not exceed 62 inches or 158 centimetres for each piece.
  • The maximum weight permissible for a single piece of baggage is 32 kgs. or 70 pounds.

Baggage Dimensions for Economy

  • The sum of the dimensions (length plus breadth plus height) of both the pieces when added together, must not exceed 107 inches. Also, the linear dimension of each piece should not exceed 62 inches.
  • The weight of each bag must not exceed 23 Kgs or 50 pounds.
